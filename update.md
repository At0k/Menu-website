# ALG Suite Website Update Plan

## Task Context

The website structure remains the same:

- Header
- Hero
- About
- Amenities
- Suites
- Transport
- Location
- Footer

The new design is a visual refresh of the existing site, with a stronger premium hospitality feel, softer color palette, and clearer section hierarchy.

## Design Goals

- Keep the current structure, but improve the visual polish.
- Make the page feel more premium, calm, and editorial.
- Improve readability and spacing on mobile.
- Strengthen conversion actions such as booking and WhatsApp contact.
- Reduce the feeling of generic placeholder sections.

## Current Design Observations

- The hero is the strongest part of the new design.
- The header is cleaner and more compact.
- The About section has a softer, centered composition.
- The Amenities and Suites sections still need more visual substance.
- The Transport section has a strong CTA moment.
- The Location section is useful but visually dense.
- The footer is more branded and feels like a proper closing block.

## Priority Update Areas

### 1. Header

- Keep the centered navigation and right-side booking CTA.
- Ensure the translucent header remains readable over the hero image.
- Make the mobile navigation clear and easy to tap.

### 2. Hero

- Preserve the big `ARTE PLUS` title treatment.
- Keep the subtitle and CTA aligned with the current premium style.
- Make sure the hero text stays readable over different images.
- Keep the floating WhatsApp action visible and unobtrusive.

### 3. About

- Keep the centered brand introduction layout.
- Improve the readability of the body copy.
- Preserve the image framing around the text block.
- Make sure the section spacing feels balanced on smaller screens.

### 4. Amenities

- Replace placeholder-looking empty areas with actual visuals or better styled content.
- Keep the split card structure, but improve balance between icon area and visual area.
- Make each amenity feel like a finished feature, not a draft block.

### 5. Suites

- Turn the suite cards into stronger visual booking cards.
- Ensure the card images or placeholders feel deliberate and polished.
- Make the suite labels and CTA feel more clickable and clear.

### 6. Transport

- Keep the dark overlay and centered CTA style.
- Make WhatsApp the primary contact action in this section.
- Keep the supporting text short and readable.

### 7. Location

- Keep the neighborhood title, map area, and distance chips.
- Reduce visual clutter where possible.
- Maintain strong spacing and clean alignment.

### 8. Footer

- Keep the purple brand band and three-column structure.
- Make sure the links and contact details remain easy to read.
- Keep the footer as a confident ending to the page.
